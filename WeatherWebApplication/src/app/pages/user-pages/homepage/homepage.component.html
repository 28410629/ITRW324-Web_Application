Welcome to Anchormen Stations

Our Mission

We, the Anchormen team, have identified a problem with weather services available today. The problem being that they prove to be not detailed enough for weather enthusiasts interest, nor provide flexibility for development or catering for the beginner DIY community. We like to resolve this problem at hand by providing a platform that will cater for these needs.

Thus, Anchormen Stations is a open-source, community driven project for creating your own cheap weather station units, using our free tools developed and guides. This allows users to view station readings from their favourite stations, being their own or other users.

Build Your Station

Located on our website sidebar, Build Station Guide, is the section containing a comprehensive check-list of the hardware and software needed to create your own weather station unit, as well as a detailed guide on how to construct assemble it.

Register Your Station

With constructed weather station units, users are able to register them on our system, found on our website sidebar Register Your Station, in turn becoming part of our weather enthusiast community, receiving readings from your own and others registered stations.

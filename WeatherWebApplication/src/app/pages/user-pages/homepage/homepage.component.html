Welcome to Anchormenstations

Anchormentstations is a open-source community driven project developing free tools for the user base, to create their own cheap weather station unit.
sdfsdfsdfsdf

Each user is able to register their own handcrafted weather station onto our website, becoming part of the community and receiving station readings from their unit.

Located on our website is a comprehensive check-list on the hardware and software needed, as well as a detailed guide on how to construct your on weather station unit

This will contain an short introduction to our project and serve as a general guide to get the user up and running :)

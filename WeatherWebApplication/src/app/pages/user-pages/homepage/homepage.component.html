Welcome to Anchormenstations


Our Mission:


We, the Anchormen team, have identified a problem with weather services available today, the problem being that they prove to be not detailed enough for weather enthuasiasts interested. Due to weather stations being expensive, we have developed our own solution.


Anchormentstations is a open-source, community driven project for creating your own cheap weather station units, using our free tools developed for our users. This allows users to view station readings from their favourite stations, being their own or other users.


Build Your Station

Located on our website sidebar, Build Station Guide is a tab leading to our comprehensive check-list of the hardware and software needed to create your own weather station unit, as well as a detailed guide on how to construct assemble it

Register Your Station

With constructed weather station units, users are able to register them on our system, found on our website sidebar Register Your Station, in turn becoming part of our weather community, receiving readings from your registered station. Each user is able to register their own handcrafted weather station onto our website, becoming part of the community and receiving station readings from their unit.
